\# BaZi Calendar Doctrine (SSOT)



\## AUTHORITATIVE CALENDAR

\- System: Solar (節氣-based), NOT lunar

\- Year, Month pillars are determined by 24 Solar Terms

\- Day pillar is continuous sexagenary cycle

\- Hour pillar derived from local apparent solar time



\## YEAR PILLAR RULE

\- Year pillar changes at 立春 (Start of Spring)

\- NOT at Gregorian Jan 1

\- NOT at Lunar New Year



\## MONTH PILLAR RULE

\- Month pillar changes strictly at solar term boundaries

\- Each solar month begins at a major solar term (節)

\- Solar longitude governs month transitions



\## DAY PILLAR RULE

\- Day pillar is uninterrupted 60-day cycle

\- Based on true local date after timezone normalization



\## HOUR PILLAR RULE

\- Hour pillar uses apparent solar time

\- Zi hour starts at 23:00 local apparent time



\## FORBIDDEN

\- Lunar calendar month for BaZi

\- Country-based calendars

\- User-facing timezone shortcuts



This doctrine is binding for all BaZi calculations.
