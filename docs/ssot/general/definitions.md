\# General Metaphysics Definitions (SSOT)



\## 1. Chart

A deterministic mapping of time, location, and system rules into a symbolic structure.



\## 2. Pillar

A pair of Heavenly Stem and Earthly Branch representing a time unit.



\## 3. Day Master (日主)

The Heavenly Stem of the Day Pillar.

This is the reference point for all BaZi analysis.



\## 4. Luck Pillar (大运)

A 10-year cycle derived from birth data and gender-specific progression rules.



\## 5. Event Chart

A chart constructed for a specific moment in time (Qi Men, Date Selection).



\## 6. Natal Chart

A chart derived from birth information; immutable.



\## 7. Determinism Rule

Given identical inputs and doctrine, output MUST be identical.

No randomness is allowed.
