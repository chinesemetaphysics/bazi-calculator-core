\# Qi Men Symbols Doctrine — SSOT



This document defines the symbols used in Qi Men and their scope.



---



\## SYMBOL CATEGORIES



\- Doors (八门)

\- Stars (九星)

\- Deities (八神)



---



\## PLACEMENT RULES



\- Symbols are placed according to:

&nbsp; - Ju number

&nbsp; - Yin/Yang Dun

\- No manual reassignment allowed



---



\## INTERACTION PRINCIPLES



\- Palace position matters more than symbol name

\- Host palace > visiting palace

\- Symbols do not act in isolation



---



\## FORBIDDEN PRACTICES



Invalid:

\- Treating symbols as standalone meanings

\- Ignoring palace context

\- Mixing systems



---



\## ENGINE CONTRACT



Engine must expose:

\- `qimen.palaces\[]`

\- `qimen.doors`

\- `qimen.stars`

\- `qimen.deities`



This doctrine is binding across all systems.
