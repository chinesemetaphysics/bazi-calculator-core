\# Flying Star Feng Shui (玄空飞星) Doctrine — SSOT



This document defines the ONLY valid Flying Star Feng Shui rules.



---



\## PURPOSE



Flying Stars analyze environmental Qi movement over time.

They do NOT analyze personal destiny.



---



\## CORE PARAMETERS



Required inputs:

\- Property facing direction (degrees)

\- Construction / last major renovation year

\- Current year / month



---



\## PERIOD SYSTEM



\- Uses 20-year periods (运)

\- Period is determined by completion date, not purchase date

\- Current system: Period 9 (2024–2043)



---



\## NATAL CHART (HOUSE CHART)



\- Constructed once per property

\- Based on:

&nbsp; - Facing

&nbsp; - Period

\- Natal stars NEVER change



---



\## ANNUAL / MONTHLY STARS



\- Overlay on natal chart

\- Influence specific sectors temporarily

\- Do not redefine house chart



---



\## INTERPRETATION ORDER



1\. Mountain Star

2\. Water Star

3\. Base Star

4\. Annual / Monthly overlays



Order is mandatory.



---



\## FORBIDDEN PRACTICES



Invalid:

\- Guessing facing direction

\- Using door direction instead of facing

\- Changing period without renovation

\- Treating stars as purely good/bad



---



\## ENGINE CONTRACT



Engine must expose:

\- `flying\_stars.period`

\- `flying\_stars.natal\_chart`

\- `flying\_stars.annual\_overlay`

\- `flying\_stars.monthly\_overlay`



---



\## NON-NEGOTIABLE



\- House chart is immutable

\- Time overlays are contextual only



This doctrine is binding across all systems.
