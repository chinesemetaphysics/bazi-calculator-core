\# Feng Shui Remedies Doctrine — SSOT



This document defines acceptable remediation logic.



---



\## PURPOSE



Remedies:

\- Harmonize Qi

\- Reduce friction

\- Support favorable flow



They do NOT:

\- Change destiny

\- Override BaZi



---



\## TYPES OF REMEDIES



\- Elemental balancing

\- Behavioral adjustment

\- Spatial reconfiguration



---



\## APPLICATION RULES



\- Remedy must match identified issue

\- Over-remedy is harmful

\- Simplicity preferred



---



\## FORBIDDEN PRACTICES



Invalid:

\- Generic “one-size-fits-all” cures

\- Excessive symbolic items

\- Fear-based selling



---



\## ENGINE CONTRACT



Engine must expose:

\- `remedies.recommended`

\- `remedies.reasoning`

\- `remedies.scope`



---



\## NON-NEGOTIABLE



\- Remedies are contextual

\- Transparency is mandatory



This doctrine is binding across all systems.
