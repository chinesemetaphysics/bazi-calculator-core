\# Ba Zhai (八宅 / Eight Mansions) Doctrine — SSOT



Ba Zhai evaluates compatibility between a person and a house.



---



\## CORE PRINCIPLE



\- Ba Zhai is PERSON-centric

\- It complements, not replaces, Flying Stars



---



\## INPUT REQUIREMENTS



\- Person's Kua number

\- House facing direction



---



\## GROUPING



\- East Group vs West Group

\- Compatibility based on matching groups



---



\## SECTORS



Eight sectors:

\- Sheng Qi

\- Tian Yi

\- Yan Nian

\- Fu Wei

\- Jue Ming

\- Liu Sha

\- Wu Gui

\- Huo Hai



Sectors indicate tendencies, not absolutes.



---



\## USAGE RULES



\- Use for:

&nbsp; - Bedroom placement

&nbsp; - Bed orientation

&nbsp; - Desk orientation

\- Do NOT use alone for renovations



---



\## FORBIDDEN PRACTICES



Invalid:

\- Using Ba Zhai without Flying Stars

\- Ignoring house chart

\- Treating bad sectors as unusable



---



\## ENGINE CONTRACT



Engine must expose:

\- `ba\_zhai.kua`

\- `ba\_zhai.group`

\- `ba\_zhai.sectors`



---



\## NON-NEGOTIABLE



\- Ba Zhai is advisory

\- Environment always outranks Ba Zhai



This doctrine is binding across all systems.
